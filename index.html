My Master’s Thesis: “50 years of urban development in Sardinia. Forms and coastal erosion impacts” was jointly supervised by University of Luxembourg and University of Sassari where I spent a 5 –month training period in the GIS Laboratory. In my research I investigate land use changes, specifically looking at urbanisation process that occurred in the period between 1954 and 2008 for two cities of Sardinia: Cagliari and Sassari. My Thesis addresses also human impact, specifically urbanisation impact on shoreline change in both coastal areas. This research helped me to practice explanatory models of urbanisation processes and machine learning using R programming. An important part of the research is a land use classification using remote sensing tools such as ERDAS for ArcGIS and developing knowledge engineering approach for panchromatic image classification.
